About The Platform

This portal is designed as the unified presentation and collaboration layer for Apollo. It provides a direct path to individual student work, technology deep-dives, and continuity with the existing Apollo paper and presentation website.

The system aligns academic rigor with production intent: traceable ownership, modular architecture, and clean roadmap expansion for ROS2 robotics fleets, landers, rovers, and autonomous farm operations.
